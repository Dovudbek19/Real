Castilla-Algeciras: gunning for a win at the Di Stéfano live on Realmadrid TV

Raúl's side play their last match of October on league matchday 10 (12:00pm CEST).

Real Madrid Castilla go into a fresh fixture at the Alfredo Di Stéfano. The squad coached by Raúl hosts Algeciras on Primera Federación matchday ten (12:00pm CEST). The madridistas will be looking to close October with a win in front of their home fans. The Andalusian side come to the Di Stéfano in 14th place and with three points more than our reserve team, thanks to their two wins, four draws and three defeats.
