Defeat in the Clásico

Real Madrid lose at the Santiago Bernabéu for the first time since April 2023.

Defeat in the Clásico, which was played at a very high tempo. The first half began with the ball moving from one area to the other and the first chance fell to the visitors after thirteen minutes in a one-on-one in which Lunin was more accurate than Lamine Yamal. Our team responded eight minutes later. Tchouameni combined with Vini Jr , who drifted into the box. skipped past Cubarsi and missed the target by inches. Inigo Martinez, didn't miss the target when he headed home Raphinha's cross on the half-hour mark. Mbappé scored in the next move, but the goal was disallowed for offside.

The tempo didn't slow down after the break, but within two minutes it was all made more difficult by Lewandowski's brace in the 54th and 56th minutes. Our team could have reduced the deficit when Mbappé' s one-on-one with Iñaki Peña, but the latter was able to parry the ball away in the 63rd minute. And Lewandowski came close to his third goal three minutes later, but hit the post with an uncontested effort.

The game was still being contested at both ends. Modrić took a free-kick in the 70th minute and Bellingham got a touch to the ball, which narrowly missed the target. In the following move, Iñaki Peña came out on top again in another one-on-one with Mbappé . And from a possible 1-2 it was 0-4 with goals from Lamine Yamal in the 77th minute and Raphinha eight minutes later.
