selling videogame franchises prized for their interactive technology, fan programs and

EA SPORTS™ F1® and EA SPORTS™ WRC in motor racing, and EA SPORTS UFC® in MMA.

players an unmatched authentic experience, bringing them closer than ever to the real thing,
with the best competitions, clubs and stars of world football. Over 19,000 players across 700
teams, more than 120 stadiums and over 30 leagues, including the full Real Madrid FC men's
and women's squads and the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium.
