easyMarkets

easyMarkets is an award-winning, highly rated financial services provider founded in
2001, headquartered in Cyprus and with offices in Australia. The company holds a range of
licenses and is regulated by the CySEC for its activity in Europe, and by the ASIC for its
operations in South East Asia and the Pacific region.

easyMarkets gives its customers access to more than 200 CFD trading and investment
instruments, including shares, currencies, indexes, industrial and precious metals, energy
and agricultural raw materials, and cryptocurrency.

Since its inception, easyMarkets has focused on offering its customers an unparalleled
trading experience through innovative tools, intuitive trading solutions and industry-
benchmark customer service. One of these innovative tools is dealCancellation, an option-
based solution which allows operators to recover funds from a losing transaction in exchange
for a small commission. Free guaranteed stop loss, negative balance protection and
guaranteed price transparency are standard protection features offered on all easyMarkets
accounts.
